Be sure to resize the game window so that the following 4 areas are visible:



A: Mission
This column holds information regarding your current mission, and gameplay tips. It is advised that you read this.

B: Terminal
This is where you enter your code to place and modify towers. Sometimes, demonstration code will be automatically added to the Terminal.

C: Grid
This is the main display for the simulation. This will provide a graphical representation for how your defenses deal with different threats.

D: Output
This is a text-based interpretation of the simulation. This window will also provide information about errors or other problems with your code.

Enemies

Normal Virus:


Often, these are referred to as just 'viruses'. These are the most basic enemy type, and the easiest to destroy. All viruses have a threat level of 1

Overclocked Virus:


These viruses require several hits to destroy, and they will accelerate every time they are attacked. All viruses have a threat level of 1

Spam:


These are not technically a threat. They are sometimes released at the same time as viruses to overwhelm your defenses. Spam has a threat level of 0

Trojan Virus:


These viruses split into several smaller and faster viruses after taking enough damage. All viruses have a threat level of 1

Packets:


These are regular files that are part of the system and must not be destroyed. Packets have a threat level of -1, since they are part of the system
